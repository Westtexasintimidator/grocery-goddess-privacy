Grocery Goddess

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2025

Overview

Grocery Goddess is a household grocery list app. We collect only the information needed to provide the service, and we never sell your data.

By using Grocery Goddess, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Grocery catalog items and shopping lists — the items, quantities, categories, and notes you add to your lists.
  • Product photos — images you upload when saving an approved product to your catalog.
  • Household membership information — your household ID and the list of member accounts in your household.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or device identifiers beyond what Firebase provides for authentication.

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store your grocery catalog and shopping lists and keep them accessible across your devices.
  • To sync your lists and catalog in real time with other members of your household.
  • To suggest a product category when you use the Quick Add feature (see Third-Party Services below).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Grocery Goddess:

  • Firebase (Google) — provides user authentication, cloud database (Firestore), and file storage for product photos. Data processed by Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — when you use the Quick Add bar, the text you type is sent to Anthropic's API to automatically suggest a grocery category and parse the item name and quantity. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

  • Your grocery data is shared only with members of your household, as chosen by you.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • We may disclose data if required by law.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Household data is retained until all members leave or the household is dissolved.

Children's Privacy

Grocery Goddess is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
